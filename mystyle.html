    Part I: Ha Long specialty
  1. Overview:
Ha Long is one of the northern tourist cities that many tourists want to visit once. Before coming here, in addition to finding out about hotels, routes, and famous places, remember to see famous Ha Long specialties as well. You won't want to miss the delicious food here. To save time, let Traveloka guide you to the culinary path in Ha Long.
  • White sticky rice with squid rolls
    • When talking about Ha Long specialties , squid rolls must be the first dish! The main ingredient is pureed squid meat, then formed into a round cake and fried. When the squid patties come out on the plate, they will have a sweet, chewy and unique flavor compared to other regions. White sticky rice with squid rolls will be the perfect combination of sticky rice grains and delicious squid rolls. Suggested address: Quang Phong Restaurant - Lot C112A Old Quarter, City. Ha Long, Quang Ninh/ Price: 35,000 - 50,000 VND/portion.

      Part II: Quang Binh specialty
    1. Overview:
    Traveling to Quang Binh, visitors will enjoy Quang Binh specialties with many different types of ingredients and cooking methods. Most notable is the source ofhigh-quality fresh Quang Binh seafood and Quang Binh dishes from the mountains, forests, streams, and streams, meticulously prepared by local chefs. Dong Hoi, the city of roses, not only has beautiful and romantic beaches, but it is also a place that provides abundant seafood. Dong Hoi people have created famous seafood dishes thanks to special favorable natural conditions.
  • Quang Binh fish and potato hotpot
    • Fish and potato hotpot is the first Quang Binh specialty to conquer discerning diners. The fish used in hot pot must be the freshest, thickest fish. The fish is gutted, headless, and tailless. It can be left whole or cut into pieces, marinated with a little salt, pepper, chili and spices, then crushed and chopped. Common ingredients for Quang Binh fish and potato hotpot are tomatoes, tamarind juice, sour star fruit, sauerkraut, sour bamboo shoots, shiitake mushrooms, etc. After the hotpot boils, add the fish until cooked, then take it out immediately, until it boils. Enjoying it immediately will give the best flavor. Eat fish and potato hotpot while it's still hot to keep the sweetness of the fish intact, without it being crushed or fishy.
